My Research Projects

Desulfurization of fuels using Solvent Extraction

I used Computational Simulation for Solvent Extraction. Further, Did Experimental verification of the best results on Self-Created Extraction Setup. I received an assistantship of INR 30000 for this Project from SNU. OUR team awarded me an "excellent" grade for my model and results from this research work. I learned that doing extensive literature work improves our research quality.

Screening of Organic Solvents for Extractive Desulfurization of Fuel Oils: DFT and MD Simulation

In this project, I used DFT theories to analyze the physical and chemical properties of the solvents. I have then calculated the interaction energies of solvents and solute for rational selection of solvent-based on their capacity and selectivity. Finally, I performed MD simulation using BIOVIA Material Studio to mimic the actual experiment and gain molecular-level insights into the solvent system.

Atomistic Simulations of Carbon Dioxide Adsorption on Hierarchical Zeolite-Supported Novel Solvents.

This is my going major project. I am using MD simulation to analyze zeolites and ionic liquids to find their capacity and selectivity in capturing carbon dioxide.
