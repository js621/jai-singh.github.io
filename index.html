My Research Projects

Desulfurization of fuels using Solvent Extraction

Computational Simulation of Solvent Extraction using Aspen Plus. Further, Did Experimental verification of the best results on Self-Created Extraction Setup. Received a grant of Rs 30,000 from SNU for this project. I was awarded a "very good" grade by OUR team

Screening of Organic Solvents for Extractive Desulfurization of Fuel Oils: DFT and MD Simulation

In this project, DFT theories to analyze the physical and chemical properties of the solvents. Then calculated the interaction energies of solvents and solute to judge solvent’s capacity and selectivity. Finally, Performed MD simulation using Biovia's material studio to cross verify the results and gained molecular level insights of the solvent system .

Carbon Capture

This is my going major project. Using MD simulation to analyze the zeolites and ionic liquid for find capacity and selectivity in capturing carbon dioxide.
